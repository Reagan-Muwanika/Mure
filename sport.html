Sports

Kyambogo University participates in various sports activities in the country organized at the University level.
Some of these activities include;

  • Football
  • Volleyball
  • Tennis
  • Basketball
  • Handball
  • Entertainment
Among others. This give the students of the Banda based university to follow their respective talent carrier path.

FootBall

The university has football teams for both gender that is men and women teams. The men's team participates in the University Football League (UFL). The team last won this League title in the 2018.
On the other hand, the women's team participates in the University Women's Football League (UWFL).
The university has its hosting ground at the Eastend of the university also known as the Teargas Arena.
It has produced some noble alumni in the country like Brian Umony who was a footballer at KCCA FC and the Uganda Cranes (Uganda National Team)



BasketBall

Kyambogo University has teams for both genders in the aspect of Basketball. These teams do train at their pitch straighted at the Eastend of the campus. These participate in both internal and external games organized at different level. Some of the alumni produced in this aspect is Henry Malinga.



Entertainment

The university has courses that support this sport activity that is Performing Artists. This gives students exposure to the various activities that transpire in the entertainment industry which include music, comedy, movie acting amongst others. Some of the noble alumni in this industry include

  • Joanita Kawalya
  • Racheal Magoola
  • Irene Ntale
  • Rema Namakula
  • Milka Irene
Amongst Others
